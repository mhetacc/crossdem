t-SNE Word Embedding Space

For each Prime Minister, the 1000 most frequent words used across their speeches are looked up in a pretrained fastText Italian embedding space and projected into 2D and 3D with t-SNE.

When Highlight overlap is on and two or more politicians are selected, words shared between the selected politicians are drawn once, in black, as a diamond marker, sitting on top of the individual traces.

t-SNE

t-distributed stochastic neighbor embedding (t-SNE) is a statistical method for visualizing high-dimensional data by giving each datapoint a location in a two or three-dimensional map. It is intended to clearly denote differences in sets and highlights potential clustering.

Word Embedding: fastText

Word embeddings are a way of representing words as vectors in a multi-dimensional space, where the distance and direction between vectors reflect the similarity and relationships among the corresponding words. Its a way to translate words into D-dimensional vectors.

fastText is an open-source, free, lightweight library that allows users to learn text representations and text classifiers. For the graph above, pre-trained word vectors were used.
